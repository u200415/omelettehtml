simple omelette recipe

An easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal.This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection,optionally filled with your choice of cheese,vegetables or meat.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Large eggs
  • Salt,to taste
  • Pepper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings:cheese,diced vegetables,coked meat,herbs

Instructions

  • Beat the Eggs: In a bowl,beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed.You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a flutter texture.
  • Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add oil or butter.
  • Cook the omelettes: Once the butter is melted and bubbling,pour in the eggs.Tilt the pan to ensure te eggs evenly coat the surface.
  • Add fillings(Optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runy in the middle,sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  • Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook,carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings.Let it cook for another one minutes,then slide it onto a plate.
  • Enjoy: Serve hot,with additional salt and pepper if needed.
